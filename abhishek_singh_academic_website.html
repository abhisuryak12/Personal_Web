// about me

Who I Am

I am Dr. Abhishek Singh, working as an Assistant Professor (Statistics) and Faculty Placement Coordinator at the Department of Mathematics and Statistics, Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT-WPU, Kothrud, Pune, Maharashtra, India, since 19 December 2022.

My work is especially focused on estimating finite population parameters using auxiliary characteristics under ranked set sampling — a cost-efficient, time-saving, and better substitute for simple random sampling.

I have introduced the first-ever neutrosophic ranked set sampling method and a generalized estimator for finite population parameters in a neutrosophic environment. I am also an approved PhD supervisor in both Mathematics and Statistics, and actively provide research internships to UG/PG students across India.

In the academic year 2024-25, I published 6 papers, and 3 more in 2025-26. I was promoted to Assistant Professor Level 11 in 2025.
